Metal Roofing in Chautauqua, NY

Serving Chautauqua County with Quality Metal Roofing

Troyer's Contracting is proud to serve homeowners and businesses throughout Chautauqua County, NY. From the historic grounds near Chautauqua Institution to lakefront cottages along Chautauqua Lake, we understand the unique roofing needs of this region.

Western New York weather demands a roof that can handle heavy snow loads, ice dams, and strong winds. Metal roofing is an ideal solution for properties in the Chautauqua area, offering decades of low-maintenance protection that stands up to the harshest winters.

Whether you own a year-round residence, a seasonal lake home, or a historic property that needs careful attention to detail, our team delivers craftsmanship you can count on. We work closely with each customer to recommend the right roofing system for their property, budget, and goals.

Our Services in Chautauqua, NY

We offer a full range of metal roofing and construction services to property owners in Chautauqua and the surrounding communities.

  • Standing Seam Metal Roofs — Clean, modern profiles with concealed fasteners and superior weather resistance.
  • Corrugated Metal Roofs — A classic, affordable option that delivers reliable protection for homes, barns, and outbuildings.
  • Metal Roof Repairs — Fast, dependable repairs for leaks, storm damage, loose panels, and worn fasteners.
  • Roof Replacement — Complete tear-off and replacement with a new metal roofing system built to last.
  • Commercial Roofing — Durable metal roofing solutions for businesses, agricultural buildings, and industrial properties.
  • Gutters & Trim — Custom gutters, downspouts, drip edge, and trim to complete and protect your roofing system.
  • Pole Barn Garages — Versatile post-frame buildings for workshops, vehicle storage, and equipment shelters.
  • Decks — Custom-built decks in pressure-treated lumber, cedar, or composite to extend your outdoor living space.
