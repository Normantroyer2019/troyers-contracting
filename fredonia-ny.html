Serving the Fredonia Community

Fredonia is one of Western New York's most distinctive villages, known for its tree-lined streets, well-preserved historic homes, and the energy that comes from being home to SUNY Fredonia. From the charming neighborhoods near Barker Commons to the residential areas surrounding the university campus, Fredonia's mix of older architecture and newer construction calls for roofing solutions that are both durable and visually fitting.

Troyer's Contracting is proud to serve Fredonia homeowners, landlords, and business owners with metal roofing that stands up to the heavy lake-effect snow and tough winters this part of Chautauqua County is known for. Whether you own a century-old Victorian in the village center or a more modern home on the outskirts of town, we provide roofing systems built to protect your property for decades.

We also work with property owners who manage rental housing near the SUNY Fredonia campus, where reliable, low-maintenance roofing is essential to keeping buildings in top shape year after year.
