Our Story

Troyer's Contracting was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses with metal roofing solutions that are built to last. What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted metal roofing contractors.

With over 15 years of experience in the metal roofing industry, we've built our reputation on hard work, honest pricing, and exceptional craftsmanship. Every roof we install reflects our commitment to quality — because we know that a roof isn't just a covering, it's the first line of defense for your home or business.

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like a neighbor. We show up on time, communicate clearly throughout every project, and stand behind our work long after the last panel is in place.
