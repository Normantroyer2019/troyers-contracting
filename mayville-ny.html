Metal Roofing in Mayville, NY

Trusted roofing and construction services for the heart of Chautauqua County

Serving Mayville and Chautauqua County

As the county seat of Chautauqua County, Mayville sits at the northern tip of Chautauqua Lake and is home to a unique mix of historic lakefront properties, year-round residences, and rural agricultural buildings. Each of these structures has distinct roofing needs, and Troyer's Contracting understands them all.

From the charming homes along the lake to the barns and outbuildings that dot the surrounding countryside, metal roofing is an ideal fit for the Mayville area. It stands up to heavy snow loads, resists the moisture that comes with lakeside living, and lasts decades longer than traditional asphalt shingles.

Whether you own a lakefront cottage, a family home in the village, or a working farm outside of town, we bring the same level of craftsmanship and attention to detail to every project.

Our Services in Mayville

We offer a full range of metal roofing and construction services to Mayville homeowners, business owners, and property managers. Here is what we can do for you:

  • Standing Seam Metal Roofs — The premium choice with concealed fasteners and a clean, modern look. Ideal for lakefront homes where weather resistance matters most.
  • Corrugated Metal Roofs — A durable, budget-friendly option perfect for homes, barns, and agricultural buildings throughout the Mayville area.
  • Metal Roof Repairs — Storm damage, leaks, or general wear and tear. We diagnose the problem and fix it right the first time.
  • Metal Roof Replacement — Upgrade your aging roof to a long-lasting metal system. We handle everything from tear-off to final inspection.
  • Commercial Metal Roofing — Large-scale solutions for businesses, retail spaces, and industrial facilities in and around Mayville.
  • Gutters & Trim — Custom-fabricated gutters and trim to complete your roofing system and protect your foundation.
  • Pole Barn Garages — Versatile post-frame buildings for workshops, vehicle storage, and equipment shelters.
  • Decks — Custom-built outdoor living spaces using quality materials to match your home and lifestyle.
