Metal Roofing in Dunkirk, NY

Durable roofing and construction services built for Lake Erie weather

Serving the Dunkirk Community

Dunkirk sits right on the shores of Lake Erie, and that location comes with real roofing challenges. Heavy lake-effect snow, ice buildup, strong winds off the water, and wide temperature swings throughout the year all take a toll on traditional roofing materials. Metal roofing is built to handle exactly these conditions, and that's what we specialize in.

Troyer's Contracting is proud to serve homeowners and businesses throughout the Dunkirk area. Whether you're in the city itself, out toward Fredonia, or anywhere along the northern Chautauqua County lakeshore, we bring the same quality craftsmanship and straightforward service to every project.

We understand what Dunkirk properties deal with season after season. Our metal roofing systems are designed to shed heavy snow loads, resist ice dam formation, and stand up to the kind of sustained wind that comes with lakefront living. When you work with us, you're getting a roof that's built for where you actually live.

Our Services in Dunkirk

We offer a full range of metal roofing and construction services to residential and commercial properties in the Dunkirk area. Every project is completed with attention to detail and materials suited to Western New York's demanding climate.

Standing Seam Metal Roofs

The premium choice for homeowners who want a clean, modern look with maximum weather protection. Concealed fasteners and interlocking seams provide a watertight seal that holds up against Lake Erie storms.

Corrugated Metal Roofs

A reliable, cost-effective option that works well for homes, barns, sheds, and agricultural buildings throughout the Dunkirk area. Strong, lightweight, and available in a range of colors.

Metal Roof Repairs

Storm damage, leaks, and wear from years of lake-effect weather don't have to mean a full replacement. We diagnose the issue and make lasting repairs that protect your property.

Metal Roof Replacement

Ready to upgrade from aging shingles or replace a worn-out roof? We handle the full process from tear-off to final inspection, leaving your property clean and protected.

Commercial Metal Roofing

From Dunkirk's downtown businesses to industrial properties along the lakeshore, we provide large-scale metal roofing solutions built for commercial demands.

Gutters & Trim

Proper water management is critical in an area that gets as much precipitation as Dunkirk. We fabricate and install custom gutters, downspouts, and trim to complete your roofing system.

Pole Barn Garages

Need extra space for vehicles, equipment, or a workshop? Our pole barn garages are built with durable post-frame construction and finished with metal roofing and siding.

Decks

Make the most of the warmer months with a custom-built deck. We work with pressure-treated lumber, cedar, and composite materials to create outdoor spaces that last.
